About Atlas

Keeping pace with global age-restricted regulations becomes increasingly complex every year. We created Atlas to simplify compliance by providing a central, public database for tracking legislation. Atlas currently tracks social media restrictions, age appropriate design codes, adult content restrictions, data privacy laws for minors, and age assurance laws impacting app stores and operating systems. Collaborative by design, Atlas allows industry professionals and regulators to contribute updates, ensuring the community always has the most accurate regulatory intelligence.

Atlas was created by Variance.
